Alg.AI
About Us
Alg.AI allows the public to be more aware of and better prepared for possible harmful algae bloom outbreaks. Our focus is on the western basin of Lake Erie, which is at greater risk for these outbreaks due to the nature of the lake. Alg.AI is a prediction tool which allows users to access accurate, long term predictions for the severity of blue-green algae blooms in various areas.

Alg.AI uses a state of the art machine learning algorithm to get results far ahead of time so that you can plan ahead for your recreation, business, or other needs.
